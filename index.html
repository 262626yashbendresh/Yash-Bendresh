Virtual Reality

Virtual Reality (VR) invented by Ivan Sutherland and his student Bob Sproull in 1990s

The use of computer modeling and simulation that enables a person to interact with an artificial three-dimensional (3-D) visual or other sensory environment is VR. This device is used for experiencing the world simply by sitting and wearing the headset. The virtual word is designed in such a way that it appears to be real using video graphics, animations, etc.

Metaverse

Metaverse is owned by no individual!

The metaverse is an immersive, interactive environment generated by a computer. Basically metaverse is a customisable computer designed environment, with metaverse one can travel virtually, and can do anything without even moving. The metaverse is expected to create new revenue streams because it will provide companies with a new way to promote goods and services, while also allowing them to collect new types of data generaged by user interactions.

Block Chain

Founded by Benjamin Reeves, Nicolas Cary, Peter Smith

Blockchain is a system of recording information in a way that makes it difficult or impossible to change, hack, or cheat the system. A blockchain is essentially a digital ledger of transactions that is duplicated and distributed across the entire network of computer systems on the blockchain.
